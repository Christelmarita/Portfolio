About

My name is Christel, and I am the founder of Onibo Design.

I call Bergen, Norway my hometown. However, I currently reside in Larvik, where I am pursuing my dreams as a UI designer. While I already have a strong foundation in this field, I am now delving into the world of front-end development to expand my skills and capabilities.

What truly ignites my passion is creating designs that are not only visually stunning but also accessible to all users. In an era where technology and design are intertwined, considering the needs of every user is crucial. By incorporating principles of universal design, I aim to break down barriers and ensure that everyone, regardless of ability, can engage with and benefit from my work.

I invite you to explore my portfolio, where you'll find a collection of my finest work. Each design is thoughtfully crafted to evoke emotion, spark curiosity, and facilitate seamless interactions.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me and my work. I am excited about the opportunity to collaborate, innovate, and create designs that make a positive impact on the world. Let's embark on this journey together and bring captivating experiences to life
